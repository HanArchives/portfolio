Front-end - Nintendo Switch

Assignment

For a school assignment I had to make a responsive website based on an existing website. I decided to go for the Nintendo website. All content is from the Nintendo website.

The assignment was to make 2 different pages of a website with vanilla HTML, CSS and Javascript. The pages need to be responsive and have a focus on the surface plane.

Progress

At first, I made a breakdown sheet of what I thought was right to use for the elements. During the process it changed because my knowledge about HTML got stronger while building the website.

I started with HTML basics for a mobile device. After that I styled the elements. When the mobile device was done, I decided to focus on the responsiveness. I put a break point at 48em and 64em so that my website is suitable for all devices. I also added dark-mode in the root for people that love dark-mode.
